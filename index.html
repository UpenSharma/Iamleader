Welcome to “I am a Leader” E-Learning page

Leadership. Find out here why it's not about your job title. It’s about your mindset!
Having joined forces with APi, a new and exciting future lies ahead of us. It’s a future that can benefit us all. And it’s a future in which each and every one of us can lead. It’s not about being a manager or heading up a team. It’s about empowering you to make your own decisions. It’s about the commitment to positively influence the people around you – whatever your role, whatever your level, wherever you are. And it’s about opening a new gateway to personal and professional development.
